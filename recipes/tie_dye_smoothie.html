Tie Dye Smoothie

Description

A visually stunning and nutritious smoothie that features layers of different colored fruit and greens. It's a fun way to enjoy a variety of fruits and sneak in some leafy greens. Customize the colors and flavors by using different fruits and food coloring. Perfect for a colorful and energizing breakfast or snack.

Ingredients:

  • Frozen bananas
  • Frozen berries (such as strawberries, blueberries, or raspberries)
  • Greek yogurt (optional)
  • Milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • Honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • Edible food coloring (assorted colors)

Steps:

  1. In a blender, combine the frozen bananas, spinach, frozen berries, Greek yogurt (if using), and milk.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy. If needed, add more milk for desired consistency.
  3. Divide the smoothie into separate bowls or glasses, one for each color you want to create.
  4. Add a few drops of different edible food coloring to each bowl or glass and stir well to create vibrant colors.
  5. Layer or swirl the different colored smoothies in a glass to achieve a tie-dye effect.
  6. If desired, drizzle honey or maple syrup on top for added sweetness.
  7. Serve with a fun straw and enjoy the colorful tie-dye smoothie!
