Rainbow Fruit Salad

Description

A vibrant and refreshing fruit salad that combines a variety of colorful fruits in a beautiful rainbow pattern. It's a healthy and delicious option packed with vitamins and antioxidants. Drizzle with honey and lime juice for an extra burst of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Strawberries
  • Oranges
  • Pineapple
  • Kiwi
  • Blueberries
  • Grapes
  • Honey (optional)
  • Lime juice (optional)
  • Fresh mint leaves (optional)

Steps:

  1. Wash and prepare all the fruits. Cut them into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Arrange the fruits in a rainbow pattern on a serving platter or bowl.
  3. If desired, drizzle honey and lime juice over the fruit salad for added sweetness and tanginess.
  4. Garnish with fresh mint leaves for extra freshness.
  5. Serve and enjoy the vibrant and delicious rainbow fruit salad!
