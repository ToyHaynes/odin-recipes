Unicorn Popcorn

Description

A vibrant and refreshing fruit salad that combines a variety of colorful fruits in a beautiful rainbow pattern. It's a healthy and delicious option packed with vitamins and antioxidants. Drizzle with honey and lime juice for an extra burst of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Popcorn kernels
  • White chocolate or candy melts (assorted colors)
  • Edible glitter or sprinkles
  • Food coloring (assorted colors)
  • Mini marshmallows (optional)

Steps:

  1. Pop the popcorn according to the package instructions and transfer it to a large bowl.
  2. Melt the white chocolate or candy melts in separate bowls following the package instructions.
  3. Add a few drops of different food coloring to each bowl of melted chocolate or candy melts and stir well to create various colors.
  4. Drizzle the different colored melted chocolate or candy melts over the popcorn, alternating colors.
  5. Sprinkle edible glitter or sprinkles over the popcorn for added sparkle.
  6. Toss the popcorn gently to coat it evenly with the melted chocolate and glitter.
  7. If desired, mix in some mini marshmallows for extra sweetness and texture.
  8. Allow the popcorn to cool and harden.
  9. Serve in colorful bowls or bags and enjoy the magical and delicious unicorn popcorn!
